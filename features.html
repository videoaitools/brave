Feature overview: campaigns, automation, CRM and messaging

Brevo positions itself as an all-in-one customer engagement platform—covering email marketing, SMS, WhatsApp, automation, CRM context, chat tools, and transactional messaging.

Campaigns Email

Email marketing

Create newsletters and lifecycle campaigns with templates, editing tools, and reporting.

  • Reusable templates & branding
  • Segmentation for targeting
  • Analytics for opens/clicks
Messaging SMS

SMS marketing

Time-sensitive updates and promotions with fast delivery and direct reach.

  • Short, high-intent messaging
  • Audience targeting and exclusions
  • Use for reminders & alerts
Messaging WhatsApp

WhatsApp campaigns

Engage where customers already chat—bulk sends plus 1:1 responses.

  • Campaign sends at scale
  • Two-way customer conversation
  • Useful for support + commerce
Journeys Automation

No-code automation

Build flows triggered by actions: signup, purchase, inactivity, page events, etc.

  • Welcome / nurture sequences
  • Behavior-based segmentation
  • Multi-step customer journeys
Context CRM

CRM-style contact view

Keep customer history and attributes in one place to personalize outreach.

  • Unified contact profiles
  • Pipeline-style tracking (for sales)
  • Notes, tasks, and status
Deliverability Transactional

Transactional messaging

Event-based emails and messages (OTP, receipts, notifications) often sent via API.

  • API + webhooks (integration-ready)
  • Templates + variables
  • Separate from marketing campaigns
