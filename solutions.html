How teams use Brevo across the customer journey

These are common patterns for “all-in-one” engagement setups: start with email, add automation, layer in SMS/WhatsApp, and connect transactional messages for real-time events.

Acquisition

Lead capture → welcome series

Convert new visitors into subscribers and guide them to first action.

    Lifecycle

    Automation journeys

    Trigger follow-ups by behavior and keep messaging consistent across channels.

    • Browse / interest signals
    • Multi-step nurture paths
    • Re-engagement campaigns
Conversion

Time-sensitive messaging

SMS/WhatsApp are often used when timing matters and attention is high.

  • Limited-time updates
  • Reminder flows
  • Support-to-sales handoff
Trust

Transactional messages

Receipts, OTP, password resets, order updates—sent via API/SMTP and templates.

  • Template variables
  • Event-based sends
  • Webhooks and delivery tracking
Retention

Win-back and loyalty

Retention focuses on consistent touchpoints and tailored offers.

  • Win-back sequences
  • Preference-based segments
  • Recurring engagement cadence
Operations

CRM-style single view

Unify the customer record so marketing, support and sales see the same context.

  • Contact history
  • Notes/tasks
  • Pipeline or status fields
